Outdoor Safety Tips

This page is dedicated to providing safety tips for outdoor adventures...

  • Always let someone know where you're going.
  • Bring adequate water and supplies.
  • Check weather conditions before heading out.
  • Stay on marked trails to avoid getting lost.
  • Be aware of wildlife and maintain a safe distance.

Leave No Trace

Leave No Trace is a fundamental rule of preserving wildlife for future generations. Here are the Seven principles:

  1. Plan ahead and prepare
  2. Travel and camp on durable surfaces
  3. Dispose of waste properly
  4. Leave what you find
  5. Minimize campfire impacts
  6. Respect wildlife
  7. Be considerate of others
