Leave no trace

Leave no trace is a fundemental rule of preserving what little bit of wildlife we have to ensure many gernerations later will be able to enjoy it as well. Below is a link to guidance on "Leave no trace"

  1. Plan ahead and prepare
  2. Travel and camp on durable surfaces
  3. Dispose of waste properly
  4. Leave what you find
  5. Minimize Campfire Impacts
  6. Respect wildlife
  7. Be Considerate of Other Visitors
