Welcome to Hiking Washington

Welcome to Hiking Washington, your ultimate guide to exploring the breathtaking beauty and rugged trails of the Evergreen State! Whether you’re a seasoned mountaineer or just starting your hiking journey, our site is designed to inspire and inform your adventures in Washington’s spectacular landscapes.

Discover stunning destinations, from the towering peaks of Mount Rainier and Mount Saint Helens to the serene paths of Olympic National Park and countless hidden gems in between. Our destination guides will help you navigate the most popular trails as well as off-the-beaten-path treasures that highlight the state’s diverse ecosystems.

Your safety is the top priority. This website provides essential safety tips and trail checklists to ensure you are prepared for any adventure—no matter the season or difficulty level. From gear recommendations and wildlife precautions to weather awareness, we’ve got you covered.

Get ready to lace up your hiking boots and experience the majestic beauty of Washington State like never before. Let’s hit the trails together—safely and sustainably!

Checklist for day hiking

  1. Water resistant jacket
  2. Water
  3. Snacks
  4. GPS or a smartphone
  5. Shoes
